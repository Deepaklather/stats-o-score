App Features
Current Matches

Keep track of score of all the matches going on in the top 3 European Soccer Leagues, English Premier League, Bundesliga and La Liga .

Rankings

Ranking tables of top 3 European Soccer Leagues at one place. Keep track of weekly changes in the ranks of your favorite Soccer teams in the top leagues of European Soccer .

Battlefield

Compare top players in Soccer world based on their FIFA stats. Want to see the stats of less popular players? No worry! We have a database of 17,000 soccer players .
